TheNisab Thresholdis automatically calculated using current real-time international gold and silver market prices.
Between the Gold Nisab or Silver Nisab threshold, many scholars prefer the Silver Nisab for Zakat. Because it allows more people to give Zakat and better supports those in need. Therefore, in our calculation, we have used the Silver Nisab threshold by default.
It is not the case that your all types of assets need to exceed 1 year separately. Rather, if only the nisab amount exceeds 1 lunar year, zakat becomes obligatory on all zakatable assets after deducting your liabilities. So, now it's time to calculate zakat precisely.
